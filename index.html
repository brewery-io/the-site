Projects

News Map

The News Map aims to visually display articles on a world map. The more a location is written about, the more prominently it appears on the map. In the works is a language processor that can also categorize the articles into categories such as environmental, economical, or political.

Foot traffic analysis

I collected foot traffic data using the Foursquare API for a handful of restaurant chains. I am correlating the traffic data with earnings from Q3 and using that to predict earnings for Q4.

BTU (Business to You)

This is an ongoing project I am tackling with a small group of fellow students. We are attempting to create a service that can crowdfund bulk goods, services, and experiences for university students. I am focusing mainly on developing the user-facing technology and in making decisions regarding operations and logistics.

Image filters

In this library, I break down popular image filters, such as the sobel edge detection filter and the gaussian blur filter using Python.

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe

This is a fun in browser two player game. It is a version of tic-tac-toe I would often play with friends in high school. To play, just share the link with a friend and enjoy.
